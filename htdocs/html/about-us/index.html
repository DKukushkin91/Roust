About us

"Welcome to Roust. We are a group of consumer-focused companies with market-leading brands in 86 countries markets around the world, including UK, USA, Germany, France, Italy, Russia, Poland and Hungary. It is with great pride that I personally ensure that Roust products are of the highest quality and offer the best possible consumer experience."

Roustam TarikoFounder and Chairman, Roust

  1. Our Mission

    Our ambition is to provide affordable luxury to everyone.

    Our goal is to build a global company of leading brands. Through consumer insight, we have a deep understanding of what our consumers need and desire. Through innovation, we create products and services that start new trends.

  2. Our Heritage

    "A leading distributor of premium spirits brands"

    Founded in 1992 by pioneering Russian businessman Roustam Tariko, Roust Inc. quickly became a leading distributor of premium spirits brands.

    1992

    Brands that reflect the new standard of quality and innovation

    Roust then set out to fulfill its founding mission by creating brands that reflect a new standard of quality and innovation. In 1998, Russian Standard Vodka was launched as Russia’s first premium vodka, quickly becoming the number one premium vodka in Russia.

    1998

    Russian leader in the international consumer goods market

    Today, Russian Standard Vodka’s brands – Original, Platinum, Gold and IMPERIA – dominate the premium vodka segment in Russia and are exported to more than 80 markets around the world. Russian Standard Vodka is Russia’s only truly global consumer brand.

    "Italy’s original sparkling wine and vermouth"

    In 2011, Roust acquired Gancia, the legendary Italian producer of sparkling wines and vermouths founded in 1850.

    2011

    "One of the largest vodka producers in the world"

    Through the acquisition of Central European Distribution Corporation, Roust became the largest integrated spirits beverage producer and distributor in Central and Eastern Europe, and one of the largest vodka producers in the world.

    2014
